Club of the Weasel Breeder
Most clubs are stout, hardwood sticks, narrow at the grip and wider at the end. This simple weapon has been used since people first began using tools. Though greatly underestimated by most, this particular club is more powerful than others of its kind. Aside from being stronger than a regular club due to its enchantments, it will occasionally bestow upon its wielder the swiftness of a weasel.

STATISTICS:

Combat abilities:
- 5% chance each hit to cast Weaselpaws on the wielder (+2 movement rate, +3 Armor Class vs. missile weapons)

THAC0: +2
Damage: 1d6+2
Damage type: Crushing
Speed Factor: 2
Proficiency Type: Club
Type: One-handed
Requires:
 5 Strength

Weight: 1

Arcane Attire of the Weasel God
This arcane attire may surely suit the needs of any adventurer. These garments, said to be blessed by the Weasel God, were crafted by the Rashemi with Theanna who, after encountering a powerful weasel spirit with the alleged ability of 'dreamwalking' - a magical art which allows the capable to enter the dreams of others - was inspired to make a magical item worthy of being named in honor of the spiritual being.

Those that wear this attire are beleived to hear confusing weasel sounds every now and then, what may affect the wearer's sanity.

STATISTICS:

Equipped abilities:
- Armor Class: +1
- Fire Resistance: +10%
- Missile Resistance: +10%
- Wisdom: -2
- May only be removed with a Remove Curse spell

Charge abilities:
- May cast Power of the Weasel God once per day. This spell temporarily grants the caster the Power of the Weasel God which, despite any first impressions suggested by the name, is in fact a powerful spirit of Rashemen with power akin to a demigod. For the duration of this spell (one turn), the caster's movement rate is increased by 12 and their armor class is increased by 2 points with an additional +5 to Armor Class vs. missiles, and they gain one and a half bonus attacks per round.

Weight: 3
